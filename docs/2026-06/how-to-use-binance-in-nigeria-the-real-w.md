## Compliance & Risk Notes

- **KYC Requirements**: Tier 1 (basic info) allows P2P and limited trading. Tier 2+ required for fiat withdrawal.
- **Regulatory Status**: Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) restricts direct NGN pairs on major exchanges; P2P remains legal.
- **Tax Reporting**: Nigerian residents must declare crypto gains; no specific CGT rate established (2026).
- **VPN Usage**: Technical access via VPN is common but may violate ToS; use residential IPs from Nigeria.

## Recommended Workflow for Nigerian Traders

1. Complete KYC on Binance (Tier 2) or Luno
2. Fund via Bank Transfer on P2P (NGN → USDT)
3. Execute trades on spot markets (USDT pairs)
4. Withdraw via Flutterwave integration or hold stablecoins for future use
5. If direct NGN needed: convert USDT → NGN via Remittance service or DEX
